Don't Starve Together

Don't Starve Together is a multiplayer survival game developed by Klei Entertainment. It's a standalone expansion of the original Don't Starve, and it places players in a dark, mysterious, and dangerous world filled with strange creatures, supernatural forces, and harsh environments.


In Don't Starve Together, you and other players are dropped into a procedurally generated wilderness with one goal: survive for as long as possible. You start with nothing and must gather resources, craft tools and shelters, and fend off both natural and supernatural threats. The world is governed by a day-night cycle and seasonal changes that significantly impact gameplay—cold winters and scorching summers can be just as deadly as monsters.


Players can choose from a roster of unique characters, each with their own strengths, weaknesses, and special abilities. Cooperation is key in multiplayer mode, although the game allows for both cooperative and competitive play styles. The tone is grim yet whimsical, with a dark Tim Burton-esque aesthetic and a constant sense of eerie danger. Ultimately, Don't Starve Together is about resource management, exploration, crafting, and surviving the ever-looming threat of starvation, madness, and death—often in that order.

Key Features:

  • Genre: Progressive Roguelike Survival Game
  • Can be Singleplayer or Multiplayer, but from the name, it is preferred to play with friends
  • It has a steep learning curve, so trying again and again to learn each time is the way for this game
  • It can be played on all PC and all consoles
